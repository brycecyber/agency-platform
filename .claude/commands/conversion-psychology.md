# CONVERSION PSYCHOLOGY — Research-Backed Rules
# Source: CRO studies, A/B test data, local business marketing research
# Every rule here is backed by real conversion data. Follow these without exception.

---

## WHAT MUST BE ABOVE THE FOLD (Before Any Scrolling)

These 6 elements MUST appear before scrolling on every site:
1. **Business name + logo**
2. **Clickable phone number** (red or high-contrast color)
3. **H1 headline** — what you do + where (e.g., "Austin's Most Trusted Plumber")
4. **One-line subtext** — key benefit or proof statement
5. **Primary CTA button** — "Call Now" or "Get Free Quote"
6. **One trust signal** — star rating, years in business, or "Licensed & Insured"

**Data:** Above-fold CTAs convert 30% higher. Above-fold content gets 57% of all viewing time.

---

## PHONE NUMBER RULES

- **Placement order by priority:** Header → Hero → After each section → CTA Banner → Footer
- **Minimum appearances:** 4 times per page
- **Always use `tel:` link** — `<a href="tel:+15551234567">`
- **Style:** Bold, large (18-24px min), high contrast — consider red
- **Mobile:** Sticky header must show phone at all times
- **90% of leads come from mobile** — 1-click calling is mandatory

## CTA BUTTON PSYCHOLOGY

### Colors That Convert (ranked)
1. **Red** — 5-34% better than green, creates urgency
2. **Orange** — 32-40% higher click rates, approachable + action
3. **Green** — Good for "safe" actions (Get Started, Book Now)
4. **Blue** — Trust/healthcare contexts

### Text That Converts
| ❌ Never Use | ✅ Always Use |
|-------------|--------------|
| Submit | Get My Free Quote → |
| Learn More | Call Now — We Answer 24/7 |
| Click Here | Schedule My Free Estimate |
| Send | See If I Qualify |
| Contact Us | Request a Callback |

**Data:** Personalized CTAs ("Get MY free quote") convert 202% better than generic ones.

### Placement Rules
- Hero: 1 dominant primary CTA (phone or quote)
- After services section: contextual secondary CTA
- After testimonials: "Join hundreds of happy customers →"
- Before footer: final conversion opportunity
- Sticky header: always accessible

### Button Sizing
- Desktop minimum: 180px wide × 48px tall
- Mobile minimum: full-width or 48px tall (thumb target)
- Never camouflage — HIGH CONTRAST to background always

## SOCIAL PROOF RULES

- **Minimum reviews to show:** 5+ (fewer hurts conversions)
- **Optimal rating to display:** 4.2-4.5 ⭐ (not suspiciously perfect)
- **Homepage testimonials:** 3-5 max (cognitive overload at 6+)
- **Each testimonial must have:** Star rating + quote + name + location/role
- **Best format:** Text + photo > text only (photos add 34% lift)
- **Video testimonials:** 80% conversion improvement — use when available

### Where to Place Testimonials
- 1 powerful one directly below hero (above the fold on some viewports)
- 1 after the services section (contextual — "here's what clients say about our [service]")
- Testimonials section (3-5 cards)
- 1 near the contact form ("see why 500+ families trust us")

## FORM OPTIMIZATION

### Optimal Fields (3-5 max)
1. Full Name
2. Phone Number (`type="tel"`)
3. Service Needed (dropdown or text)
4. Message/Details (optional textarea)
5. *(Optional)* Best time to call

### Submit Button Text
- "Send My Request →"
- "Get My Free Quote →"
- "Schedule My Free Estimate →"
- "Request a Callback →"

### Below the Form (reduces anxiety)
```html
<p class="text-xs text-center text-gray-400 mt-2">
  🔒 We'll call you back within 24 hours. No spam, ever.
</p>
```

### Form Layout Rules
- Single column (easier to scan on mobile)
- Labels ABOVE inputs (not placeholder-only)
- Large tap targets (48px height minimum)
- Real-time validation feedback
- Never ask for more than needed

## MOBILE RULES (Non-Negotiable)

- **60-75% of local business traffic is mobile**
- Minimum font size: **16px** (prevents iOS auto-zoom on inputs)
- All tap targets: **minimum 48×48px**
- Phone number: **always `tel:` link, always in sticky header**
- Hero text: **readable without zooming at 375px width**
- Form: **usable with thumbs, not too wide**
- Page speed: **under 3 seconds or lose 50% of visitors**
- Hamburger menu: **always functional**

## NAVIGATION BEST PRACTICES

- **5-7 items maximum** in desktop nav
- **Sticky header** = +10% more conversions (proven)
- **Phone number in top-right** of nav always
- **Primary CTA button in nav** ("Call Now" or "Get Quote")
- **Hamburger on mobile** with all same links + phone prominent

### Optimal Desktop Nav Items
```
[Logo]  Services  About  Reviews  Service Area  FAQ  [📞 Call: (555) 000-0000]
```

## FOOTER MUST-HAVES (Local SEO + Conversions)

Every footer needs:
1. **NAP** — Name, Address, Phone (exact match to Google Business Profile)
2. **Hours of operation**
3. **Service area cities listed**
4. **All service links** (with city modifier if possible)
5. **Social links**
6. **License/BBB badges**
7. **Copyright year**

**Why NAP matters:** Google uses footer NAP to confirm local business legitimacy for rankings.

## FAQ SECTION — Objection Handling

FAQs go **before the final CTA**, not buried on a separate page.

### Universal FAQ Questions for Local Businesses
1. Do you offer free estimates?
2. Are you licensed and insured?
3. How quickly can you respond?
4. What areas do you serve?
5. Do you offer emergency service?
6. How much does [service] cost?
7. What payment methods do you accept?
8. What happens if I'm not satisfied?

**Data:** FAQ sections reduce sales objections and increase contact form submissions. 90% of purchase decisions are emotional — FAQs address emotional barriers.
